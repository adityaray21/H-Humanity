Our Causes

The main cause behind this initiative are many but the main reason being that we want to fulfill as many wishes as we can of underpriviliged people.

Water scarcity

Children in 100 million homes in India lack water. 21% of the country's diseases are water-related. If steps not taken, water scarcity is expected to become a leading cause of national political conflict in the future. A group of people can install water purifiers in different areas.

Education

Due to inadequate financial conditions of the family, Many children work in factories, stores, stalls, etc. to feed their parents. If we all give them time for tuitions every weekend or thrice a week, it would definitely help them.

Street dogs

Around 1000 stray dog dies because of road accidents every year. The actual number could be much more because many people don't bother informing the MC whenever they accidentally hit an animal. Most people don't consider it their personal responsibility to help an injured animal. If every one of us feeds and helps street dogs near our society, the numbers can be changed shortly.

Shelter

According to the 2011 census, there are 1.77 million homeless people in India. And many of them die due to diseases, cold weather, hunger etc. If every one of us provides them with vaccines, blankets, foods, and other items. We can save many lives.
